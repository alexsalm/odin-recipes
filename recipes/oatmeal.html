Oatmeal

Description

This recipe uses steel-cut oats, water, and blueberries to create a wonderful oatmeal dish. A tablespoon of peanut butter can be added after heating for those who seek a higher protein content.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup steel-cut oats
  • Blueberries
  • 8-9 ounces of water

Steps

  1. Add steel cut oats to a medium-sized bowl
  2. Add water
  3. Microwave on oatmeal setting (SO EASY)
  4. Sprinkle blueberries after microwaving

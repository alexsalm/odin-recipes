Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

This is a classic. Who doesn't like a nice peanut butter and jelly sandwhich? Opt for whole wheat for more fiber and sustained energy.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (white or whole wheat)
  • 1 tsp jelly
  • 1 tsp peanut butter

Steps

  1. Sread jelly on one side of slice of toast.
  2. Set aside with jelly facing upwards.
  3. Spread peanut butter one one side of second slice of toast.
  4. Set aside with peanut butter facing upwards.
  5. Combine both slices with jelly and peanut butter by pressing against eachother.
